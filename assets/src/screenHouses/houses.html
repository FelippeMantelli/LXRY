Travel up the floating staircase or elevator to the remaining 4 ensuite bedrooms and spacious primary suite. Additional features in this home include engineering oak flooring, onyx stone, and limestone throughout. Not to be overlooked is the 3-car garage with option for lifts and an indoor garage with glass enclosure to show off your finest car. This is a property that you do not want to miss!

LA PALMERAIE 2 HARBOUR ISLAND, BAHAMAS

“La Palmeraie 2” sits on the pinnacle of savoir island lifestyle within the 'Nantucket of the Caribbean', Harbour Island. The villa canopies on a 20-foot dune over a spectacular stretch of the famous three-mile Pink Sand Beach”. La Palmeraie #2 with its sleek ultra modern design combines an open-concept, bright, and breezy indoor living with the freedom to play outside with 110 feet of undisturbed pink sand beach frontage.

La Palmeraie is acclaimed as one of the most successful vacation rentals on the island. Homeowner’s take advantage of the island’s rental market due to limited beachfront options available and no rental restrictions.br

La Palmeraie #2 was built and engineered in 2021 to the highest Floridian specifications with 110 feet of undisturbed Pink Sand Beach. Per linear feet, Harbour Island is known for being the most expensive beachfront in The Bahamas with extremely limited options available on the beach.

Wide, stone steps whisk you away to the grand entrance to stunning 23-foot vaulted ceilings and offering 11,736 square feet of living space. Take a stroll along the pool deck to the stunning pool, which is slightly raised to blend in with the shimmering ocean while you swim laps. Villa features options for al fresco dining with breathtaking views while being covered.

Slumber in style within the villa’s 9 bedrooms and 9.5 bathrooms each a masterpiece of design adorning state-of-the-art modern amenities, plush bedding, floor-to-ceiling glass windows and perfectly positioned panoramic views from dawn to dusk. The villa's open designed nine en suite bathrooms all offer exceptional European fittings and custom tile work. Among spectacular amenities include the 55-foot heated infinity-edge pool facing the ocean, 1,500-square-foot rooftop deck with an eight-person Jacuzzi, and outdoor kitchen. The view is unlike any other on Harbour Island with a 365 degree vista over the Island. Soak in the sunrise over the water or the sunset over the marina. Among the many features also include a massage room, an outdoor shower, Sonos sound system a private spa and steam room, a private cinema and a fully equipped gym.

Be welcomed into a historic community accessed through bright bougainvillea lined, quaint narrow roads passing pastel-coloured cottages, fantastic restaurants, shops, houses and boutique hotels.
